HOW TO PLAY
1. Pick a car

Choose a manufacturer, search a model, or use the class filter.

2. Study stats

Top speed, horsepower, acceleration, handling, braking, engine, fuel, and weight show in the garage.

3. Earn Inficoins

Win races and complete achievements. Each achievement pays 100 Inficoins.

4. Upgrade

Buy engine, turbo, tire, brake, and weight upgrades. Upgraded cars save to Favorites.

5. Drive

Use W/A/S/D or arrow keys, mouse drag for camera, C for camera mode, and R to reset.

6. Race CPU

Open the simulator, choose Drag Strip, pick a CPU car, and race a full mile.
